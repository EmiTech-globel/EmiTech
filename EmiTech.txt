Web Design
Learn the fundamentals of creating 
beautiful and user-friendly websites,
including HTML, CSS, and basic design
principles.

Web Development
Master both back-end and front-end development 
with the latest technologies like JavaScript, 
React, Node.js, and database management.

Graphic Design
Explore the basics of graphic design,
including color theory, typography,

and Illustrator.

Branding & Identity <br> Design
